Addiction may cause other medical conditions

Addiction is a disease - Do not be ashamed

Alcohol or drug dependency disorder is a disease. Our patients learn that addiction is a physical, mental and spiritual illness. Our task is to provide them with the necessary tools and support to enable them to take responsibility of their recovery.

 

Learn More Watch Video
Step 1 - Receive Patient

Once we receive a patient, the nursing team runs initial medical tests to prioritise the process of care.

Step 2 - Screening

A qualified counselor undertakes a screening process using standardised tools to structure a care plan.

Step 3 - Medical Analysis

The resident doctor runs medical tests to treat underlying conditions (if any) caused by addiction.

Step 4 - Treatment

The care programme commences and the patient is assisted to adjust to the recovery process.
